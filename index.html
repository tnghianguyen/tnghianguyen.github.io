About me

I am from Vietnam and my Vietnamese name is Nguyễn Trung Nghĩa. I am currently in the process of graduation at VNUHCM-University of Science (VNUHCM-US), where I defended my senior thesis on July 2021 under the supervision of Professor Mai Hoang Bien.

Research

Publications

  1. (with L.Q. Danh and N.K. Ngoc) Subpermutable subgroups of skew linear groups and unit groups of real group algebras, Bull. Korean Math. Soc. (2021). Link

Talks/Presentations

  1. In August 2021, I delivered an online talk on brief introduction to tensor categories in the language of string diagrams in the Workshop "Tannakian Categories" hosted by Professor Phung Ho Hai and sponsored by Hanoi Institute of Mathematics.
  2. In December 2020, I presented a new simple result that every map on the ring of matrices on a division ring is additive iff it is additive on the general linear group at the 12th VNUHCM-US Scientific Conference.
  3. In September 2020 at the REU Symposium, I gave a talk on basic theory of affine group schemes.

Teaching

Starting this fall, I am starting as a teaching assistant to MTH00086 Discrete Mathematics at VNUHCM-US. Before that, I was one of the two mentors to the selected team of VNUHCM-US to enter Vietnam Mathematical Olympiad for Undergraduates. Indeed, I was completely in charge of giving lectures on problem-solving strategies on linear and abstract algebra. Unfortunately, the contest has been cancelled due to the surging cases of COVID-19 in Southern Vietnam.

Apart from that, I have been an enthusiastic long-standing member of Projects in Mathematics and Applications (PiMA) where I have been serving as multiple pivotal roles such as an organizer, a counselor and a mentor.

Other activities

In Spring 2021, I co-ran a student learning seminar on the character theory of finite groups.
