Teaching and Outreach

  • In Fall 2021, I was a teaching assistant to MTH00086 Discrete Mathematics at VNUHCM-US.
  • In Spring 2021, I was one of the two instructors of the selected team of VNUHCM-US to enter Vietnam Mathematical Olympiad for Undergraduates, giving lectures on problem-solving strategies in linear and abstract algebra.
  • Apart from that, I have been an enthusiastic long-standing member of Projects in Mathematics and Applications (PiMA) where I have been serving as multiple pivotal roles such as an organizer, a counselor and a mentor.
